Shanghai, China

I come from China and I live in Shanghai.


Location and climate

  • Location:Shanghai is located in the middle of the east Coast of China and the easternmost part of the Yangtze River Delta, with the East China Sea in the east, Hangzhou Bay in the south, and Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces in the west. Chongming Island at the north is at the mouth of the Yangtze River. Together with the surrounding cities of Jiangsu, Zhejiang and Anhui provinces, Shanghai forms a world-class Yangtze River Delta urban agglomeration.
  • Climate:Shanghai has a subtropical monsoon climate with four distinct seasons, plenty of sunshine and rainfall. The climate is mild and humid, with shorter spring and autumn and longer winter and summer.

Famous view

  • The Oriental Pearl Tower:The Oriental Pearl Tower is one of the landmark cultural landscapes of Shanghai. It can be said that it has witnessed the rise and fall of Shanghai since the reform and opening up. The tower has space capsule, rotating restaurant, exhibition hall of Shanghai city history development and other landscape and facilities, the tower has a wide view, can overlook the scenery of the Huangpu River.
  • The Bund:The Bund is the most famous place in Shanghai. The street scene of the Bund is the most characteristic of Shanghai with its river, long embankment, green belt and magnificent architectural complex. When the lights come on, the buildings on the Bund are brightly lit, so that tourists at home and abroad are amazed.
  • The Shanghai World Financial Center:The Shanghai World Financial Center is a skyscraper in Lujiazui, with a height of 492 meters and 101 floors on the ground. It looks like a sharp sword thrust into pudong from afar. At the Pavilion, located on the 100th floor of the building, you can feel the roof of the Jin MAO Tower under your feet, like walking in the clouds, and enjoy the panoramic view of the huangpu River.

Delicous food

  • Born Fried steamed bun:Shengjian mantou, a homegrown dim sum in Shanghai, is said to have a history of more than 100 years. Sheng jian consists of semi-fermented flour wrapped in fresh meat and meat jelly, placed in rows in a pan, doused with cold water several times during the frying process, and finally sprinkled with scallions and sesame seeds.
  • Small wonton with three delicacies:Shanghai's three Delicacies wonton is also different from wuxi's. Wonton fillings are not made from fresh meat, foreign vegetables or pickled mustard, but pure meat. The so-called three delicacies are all in the soup, the egg, shrimp skin, laver three delicacies of the fresh meat wrapped in thin skin, taste salty and smooth.
